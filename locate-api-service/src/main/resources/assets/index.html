Register to vote

Not what you're looking for? ↓

Use this service to add or update your details on the electoral register.

Your details need to be on the electoral register (sometimes called the 'electoral roll') before you can vote in UK elections and referendums.

Before you start

Registering takes around 5 minutes

You’ll need your National Insurance number (if you have one). If you’re living abroad, you may also need your passport.

Register to vote

Register by post

To apply by post, download and complete the relevant form. Or contact your local Electoral Registration Office and ask them to post a copy to you.
